Forbes Lake

Non-Operated Working Interest, Illinois

Non-Operated • ~21% Working Interest

A Proven Asset

BGI Forbes Lake, LLC holds approximately a 21% working interest in an oil field beneath Forbes Lake State Park in Illinois. All wells were drilled directionally from locations off the park boundary to access a producing formation under the lake.

The Forbes Lake field has been in BGI's portfolio for approximately 20 years and has produced over 4.58 million barrels of oil cumulatively across 14 wells. The field produces from multiple zones including the Geneva B, McClosky, Benoist, Salem, and Aux Vases formations.

Active Wells

2025 Highlight: Warren 1 Returns

Warren 1 is historically the field's most prolific producer, with over 2.29 million cumulative barrels. It went offline in mid-2024 due to a downhole casing failure. A successful workover in early 2025 brought it back online.

The well ramped from near-zero production in January to over 60 barrels per day by mid-year, producing 13,110 barrels in 2025 alone.

Future Opportunity

Geneva A Zone

There is an identified opportunity to drill at least one new well targeting the Geneva A zone, which has never been produced near the active field area. Warren 1 alone has produced 2.3 million barrels from the Geneva B, so this zone represents real upside. It would require raising capital for exploration.
